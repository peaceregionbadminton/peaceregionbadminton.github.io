About Us

Peace Region Badminton and Recreational Club is a registered institution working towards the betterment of the citizens of the Peace Region by providing health-oriented recreational activities, taking care of the mental as well as physical health of our members.

Who Are We?

We are a team of highly motivated and trained individuals promoting sports and recreational activities. Our club started off as a badminton training facility for young talent interested in learning the sport. We are currently expanding our services to include a broader range of sports and other recreational activities to be able to provide our citizens with a wide range of options to choose from.

Mission

We strive to promote sports and a healthy lifestyle while taking care of your recreational needs. Our mission is to provide our members with a platform to practice sports and encourage our people to learn new sports. We want to spearhead our patrons to try new sports and provide the interested ones the facilities to master any game they want!

Vision

Our visionary leaders have a dream of a healthier and a more content society. In the coming years, we want to provide a wide number of services to our members. We are working day and night to fulfil our dream of building a community of health-loving and sports enthusiasts. In a not so far future, we aim to build an unprecedented sports establishment in the Peace Region for members of all age groups offering other sport starting with bubble balls.

Badminton

Our expertise remains in proving world-class training through internationally acclaimed methodologies of Badminton training. Our team includes international player. Either you are a pro or just a starter, we have various programs designed as per your personal needs and requirements.

Bubble Ball

In bubble ball you will be wrapped in a big plastic inflatable ball with handles and shoulder straps. Bubble balls are perfect to make your event joyful. Book your bubble balls today for birthday parties, corporate events, team building, church groups, family reunions, schools, fundraisers! This recreational activity is the first of its kind available in the Peace Region. We provide you with equipment and the playground to spend quality enjoyable time with your family and friends. We even have other options to rent the equipment, in case you want to try the game somewhere else, or even in your backyard!
